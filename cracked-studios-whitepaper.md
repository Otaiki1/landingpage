## 1. Executive Summary

Cracked Studios is a **human-AI hybrid web3 studio** building the next generation of decentralized games, consumer applications, and developer tooling. We are redefining what a modern studio looks like — not just in what we build, but in *how* we operate.

**What makes us different:** We are not a studio that uses AI. We are a studio that is *built around* AI — a human-AI organism executing at startup speed with enterprise-grade intelligence infrastructure.

## 2. Company Identity & Repositioning

### 2.1 Who We Are

Cracked Studios is a **Lagos, Nigeria-based web3 multi-product studio** with a global audience mandate. We build games, consumer apps, and developer tooling that are:

- **Easy to enter** — frictionless onboarding, hybrid wallet options, Web2-grade UX
- **Fun to play** — social loops, addictive mechanics, competitive stakes
- **Rewarding to use** — real ownership, bounties, on-chain rewards
- **Culturally resonant** — meme-native, Gen-Z-aligned, content-first

### 2.2 The Repositioning

Traditional studios are built around headcount. More products require more people. More people require more capital. More capital requires traction, which requires more people. It is a slow, expensive, fragile loop.

**Cracked Studios breaks that loop.**

We have restructured the company as a **human-AI hybrid organism** — the three founders act as operators, strategists, and decision-makers; The Rig acts as the permanent intelligence and execution layer that handles knowledge management, content generation, community intelligence, revenue tracking, analytics, and operational continuity.

This repositioning has three consequences:

1. **We move faster** — The Rig processes information and generates outputs continuously, without context-switching delays.
2. **We stay sharper** — Every Signal reads from a shared knowledge base (The Stack), ensuring every function of the company is always operating on the same truth.
3. **We scale asymmetrically** — Adding a new product does not require hiring a new team. It requires a new Signal and updated Stack entries.

This is not a future vision. The Rig is live. The Stack is live. The Signals are operating today.

### 2.3 Philosophy

> *Lower the barriers. Amplify the vibes. Keep people coming back.*

We believe adoption in web3 is not primarily a technology problem — it is a culture and experience problem. People do not avoid crypto games because of the blockchain. They avoid them because the onboarding is hostile, the UX is confusing, and the culture is inaccessible.

We fix all three — simultaneously.

## 3. The Rig — Human-AI Operating Architecture

### 3.1 What The Rig Is

The Rig is Cracked Studios' internal AI agent infrastructure — a structured network of eleven AI agents, each called a **Signal**, each responsible for a specific business function. Together, the Signals form the intelligence backbone of every product we ship.

The Rig runs on **The Stack** — a private GitHub repository that serves as the single source of truth for all Signals. Every Signal reads from `/core-stack/` (shared company context) and its own `/signal-memory/` folder (domain-specific knowledge), then writes outputs to `/drop-zone/` where other Signals can consume them.

This creates a **living, self-updating intelligence network** where every business function is always in sync.

### 4.2 Blokaz — Live Product (Celo Mainnet)

Blokaz is Cracked Studios' first **fully live, deployed product** — an on-chain block puzzle game (tetromino-style, 9×9 grid) where every game session is cryptographically committed to the blockchain, scores are independently verifiable without trusting a server, and prizes are distributed automatically in USDC via smart contract. There is no native token, no speculation, and no server that can be manipulated.

**Live deployment:** Celo Mainnet | **Upcoming:** MiniPay (Celo's default embedded wallet, serving millions of users) | **Distribution:** Farcaster MiniApp | **Website:** [blokaz.xyz](https://www.blokaz.xyz)

**Core Mechanics:**
- **Classic Mode (free):** The top-of-funnel — unlimited play with no entry fee, weekly score tracked on an on-chain leaderboard
- **Tournament Mode (paid):** Entry fees of $0.50–$50+ in USDC, prize pools auto-distributed by smart contract, `finalizeTournament()` is a public function anyone can call
- **Epoch System:** Weekly leaderboard resets create a built-in recurring engagement loop

**Why Blokaz is architecturally significant:**
Most Web3 games claim fairness but rely on off-chain servers for score validation. Blokaz commits game sessions cryptographically on-chain *before* play begins. The outcome is provable, not trusted. This is a genuine technical differentiator — other prize gaming platforms (Skillz, Web3 RPGs) cannot claim this without significant reengineering.

**Revenue Model:**
```
Tournament Prize Pool  =  Σ(all entry fees)
Protocol Revenue       =  Prize Pool × 5%
Weekly Reward Pool     =  Prize Pool × 5%
```
Revenue is collected at the smart contract level the moment a player joins a tournament — no invoicing, no payment processor, no human intervention.

### 4.5 BitDrum — Vault-Backed BTC Prediction Markets (Somnia Testnet → Mainnet)

BitDrum is a **decentralized protocol for short-duration Bitcoin price direction prediction markets**, deployed on the Somnia EVM blockchain. It is live today on Somnia Shannon Testnet and is on the mainnet roadmap.

**Website:** [bitdrum.xyz](https://bitdrum.xyz) | **Chain:** Somnia Shannon Testnet (Chain ID 50312) | **Status:** Live, keeper automated, demo-ready

**The Core Question BitDrum Answers:**
> *Will Bitcoin's price be higher or lower than it is right now, in 1 minute or 5 minutes?*

Users stake native STT tokens on UP or DOWN. A **protocol-owned liquidity vault** automatically matches every stake on the opposite side — guaranteeing a counterparty for every trade instantly, with no bootstrapping problem. Winners receive their stake plus a dynamic profit bonus (30–80%) determined by a transparent, vault-health-based formula.

**What Makes BitDrum Architecturally Significant:**

| Problem | BitDrum Solution |
|---------|-----------------|
| Counterparty dependency (Polymarket/Kalshi model) | Vault-backed fills — every trade fills instantly |
| Long resolution times (days/weeks) | 60-second and 300-second markets |
| UX friction (approvals, wrapping) | Single payable transaction in native STT — no approvals |
| Opaque/manipulable price feeds | Keeper-fed on-chain adapter, updated every 20s |
| Hidden payout rates | Deterministic vault-health formula, visible before confirmation |

**Why Somnia:**
Somnia processes 1M+ TPS with sub-second finality. For a 60-second prediction market, this is not optional — it is essential. A 1-minute market cannot tolerate 10-second block confirmation times. BitDrum uses Somnia's Reactivity SDK for validator-level push events and Streams SDK to publish AI signals as on-chain append-logs.

**The AI Signal Agent — "The Core":**
An optional Python/FastAPI advisory layer that generates directional signals (UP/DOWN/NEUTRAL) with confidence scores and natural-language rationale. Signals are published to Somnia Streams — making them verifiable and composable for any external dApp. Critically: The Core is advisory only. It does not control settlement or payout rates. The protocol is fully functional without it.

**Economic Model — Structurally Profitable Vault:**

```
Tournament Prize Pool  =  User Stake + Vault Match (1:1)
Protocol Fee           =  Total Pool × 2%  →  TreasuryV2
Vault Net (any outcome) =  Always positive below 80% payout rate
```

The vault is structurally profitable at any payout rate below 100%. Even if the vault loses every single market, the 2% fee and sub-100% payout ceiling guarantee a net surplus. This is not dependent on win rate.

| Payout Rate | Vault Net (Win) | Vault Net (Loss) | Expected Net (50/50) |
|-------------|-----------------|------------------|----------------------|
| 30% (min) | +1.96 STT | +0.66 STT | +1.31 STT |
| 50% | +1.96 STT | +0.46 STT | +1.21 STT |
| 80% (max) | +1.96 STT | +0.16 STT | +1.06 STT |

**Smart Contract Architecture (V2 — Fully Deployed):**
Six contracts on Somnia EVM: `BitdrumPriceAdapter`, `PredictionMarketV2`, `LiquidityVaultV2`, `SettlementEngineV2`, `TreasuryV2`, `LeaderboardRegistry`. All deployed, keeper automated, all contract addresses publicly verifiable on Somnia Shannon Explorer.

### 4.7 Noah Protocol — Identity Infrastructure (Testnet Live, Mainnet Q4 2026)

Noah Protocol is Cracked Studios' infrastructure play — a **privacy-preserving, zero-knowledge identity layer for Web3 and Web2 applications**. It solves one of the most expensive, friction-heavy problems in the entire crypto ecosystem: KYC and identity verification.

**Website:** [noahprotocol.xyz](https://www.noahprotocol.xyz) | **Live:** Starknet Testnet | **Mainnet:** Q4 2026

**The Problem Noah Solves:**
Identity verification is broken across Web3 in three compounding ways. Users upload their passport repeatedly across every platform (80% onboarding drop-off). Developers spend millions building compliance infrastructure from scratch. And identity systems are completely siloed — a user verified on one platform is a stranger on the next.

**Noah's Solution — Verify Once, Use Everywhere:**
Noah uses ZK-SNARK proofs to let users scan their passport locally in the browser, generate a cryptographic proof without transmitting personal data, and bind that proof to their wallet. The result is a **portable, reusable identity credential** that works across any dApp or Web2 platform — with zero personal data ever stored on Noah's servers.

The five-step flow:
```
Scan Passport (browser) → Generate ZK Proof (local) → 
Submit Proof On-chain → Bind Wallet Address → 
Reuse Credential Across Any dApp
```

**Technical Architecture:**
- ZK-SNARK proofs generated client-side — nothing leaves the user's browser
- Nullifier system ensures one passport = one wallet (100% sybil resistance)
- Avalanche native; now expanding to Starknet and designed multi-chain from the ground up
- REST API layer for Web2 integration — no blockchain knowledge required
- Compatible with MetaMask, WalletConnect, Privy out of the box

**Hackathon Validation:**
Noah placed **7th in the Privacy Track at the Starknet Re{define} Hackathon 2025**, competing against top ZK and identity projects globally. This is third-party technical validation from one of the most credible blockchain ecosystems in the space.

**Developer Value Proposition:**

**Use Cases:**

## 13. Critical Analysis — Feasibility Assessment

*This section presents an honest, rigorous assessment of the Cracked Studios business model. Transparency about risks and challenges is as important as presenting the opportunity.*

---

### 13.1 What the Model Gets Right

**✅ The Human-AI Hybrid Structure is Genuinely Novel in This Space**

No comparable web3 gaming studio in the African market — and very few globally — operates with a structured, domain-separated AI agent layer. This is not a cosmetic advantage. It directly affects speed, consistency, and cost efficiency in ways that compound over time. The architectural decision to embed AI at the operating model level (not just use AI tools ad-hoc) is a legitimate structural moat.

The distinction matters: any company can say "we use AI." Almost none can say "our AI agents have domain-specific memory, read from a single source of truth, and write outputs that feed into each other." That is a system. Systems are hard to replicate.

**✅ The Multi-Product Ecosystem Model is Correctly Timed**

The crypto gaming market of 2021–2022 was dominated by single-product plays — Axie, StepN, and others. Most collapsed when token prices dropped because they had no ecosystem to catch users who left one product. A multi-product architecture with shared infrastructure and cross-product LTV is the evolved, more resilient model. Cracked is building this from day one.

**✅ Tweetle Provides Genuine Validation**

170 users with 98 transactions each is behavioral evidence of product love, not vanity metrics. In early-stage consumer products, depth of engagement at this scale is a meaningfully better PMF signal than most pre-seed web3 companies can demonstrate. The challenge is scaling it — but the foundation is real.

**✅ The African Market Timing is Correct**

Nigeria's crypto adoption rate, youth demographic size, and mobile-first digital culture create a rare combination: a population that is simultaneously underserved by quality gaming products AND crypto-native enough to use on-chain products without heavy hand-holding. Cracked's local fluency + global product ambition is a strategic advantage that a US or European studio would struggle to replicate authentically.

**✅ Revenue Model is Appropriately Conservative**

The decision to prioritize gameplay quality before aggressive monetization, use cosmetics/passes rather than P2E tokens, and tie monetization to engagement rather than token speculation is the correct lesson from the 2022 web3 gaming crash. The model is built to survive bear markets.

---

### 13.2 Honest Challenges & Tensions

**⚠️ The Rig's Quality Depends on Maintenance Discipline**

The Stack Sync (30-minute weekly updates) is the lifeline of The Rig's relevance. If the founding team falls behind on updates during intense product sprints — which is likely — Signal outputs will drift from current reality. Stale knowledge produces stale intelligence. This is a process discipline risk, not a technology risk, but it is real and must be planned for.

*Recommended Mitigation: Automate Stack Sync triggers wherever possible. Build structured update templates that reduce cognitive load. Designate rotating Sync Warden responsibility.*

**⚠️ Multi-Product Pipeline is a Double-Edged Sword**

Six products in various stages of development with three founders and a $50K raise is genuinely ambitious. The ecosystem model is compelling in theory, but there is a real risk of spreading thin. If Tweetle's relaunch absorbs all bandwidth and Neon Sentinel slips, the "pipeline" narrative begins to look like a list of promises.

*Recommended Mitigation: Hard prioritization. Tweetle relaunch and Neon Sentinel are the two sequenced priorities. All others must have clearly gated milestones tied to revenue triggers, not calendar dates.*

**⚠️ AI Agents Amplify Human Judgment — They Do Not Replace It**

The Rig generates outputs. Humans must still evaluate, approve, and act on them. If the team over-relies on Signal outputs without sufficient critical review, decision quality degrades. The Rig is as good as the prompts, knowledge, and human oversight behind it.

*Recommended Mitigation: Define clear human approval gates for every Signal's output type. Flare content should not post without human review. Vanta revenue recommendations should not be actioned without founder sign-off. Monthly Signal quality audits.*

**⚠️ Web3 Gaming Has a Credibility Problem**

The broader market — users and investors alike — carries scar tissue from 2021–2022. Many web3 gaming companies made large promises and delivered little. Cracked Studios must work harder than a comparable Web2 studio to establish trust.

*Recommended Mitigation: Lead with gameplay and experience before leading with blockchain mechanics. Let users fall in love with the game first. Tweetle's relaunch messaging should be game-first, crypto-second.*

**⚠️ The $50K Raise is Structurally Thin**

At 45% developer costs ($22,500), this provides approximately 3–4 months of developer runway depending on compensation structures. If milestones slip — as they often do at pre-seed — the team could reach a fundraising moment without sufficient traction for a meaningful next round.

*Recommended Mitigation: Pursue non-dilutive grant funding aggressively in parallel via Thread. Layer in early revenue signals from NFT Pass and tournament fees as quickly as possible. Maintain a no-excess-burn discipline throughout.*

---

### 13.3 Feasibility Verdict

**Overall Feasibility: HIGH — with disciplined execution.**

The model is sound. The market is real. The execution evidence is extensive and multi-chain: Blokaz live on Celo, Uzoza live on Base, Otterpay live on Starknet Mainnet, BitDrum live on Somnia testnet, Noah live on Starknet testnet — all built by the same small team, all pre-institutional funding. Five products across five chains.

This is the strongest pre-seed execution story in the African web3 space today. The risk is not "can they build?" — that question is definitively answered. The risk is prioritization and runway management across a rich portfolio. The founders must be ruthless about which two products receive full focus at any given time, and let The Rig carry the operational load for the rest.
